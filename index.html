How to Fix Common Trézor Suite Issues

Although Trézor Suite is highly reliable, users may encounter minor issues. Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Ensure Software is Updated – Keep Trézor Suite and firmware up to date.
  2. Restart Your Device – A simple reboot can resolve temporary bugs.
  3. Check USB Connections – Use the original cable and try different ports.
  4. Disable Conflicting Software – VPNs, antivirus programs, or browser extensions may interfere with functionality.
